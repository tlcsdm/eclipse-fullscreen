The Fullscreen Preference Page

You can configure various preferences to trim Fullscreen to your liking. The preference section can be reached via Window - Preferences - General - Full Screen. Fullscreen has these settings:

  • Hide Menu Bar in Full Screen Mode: Hides the main menu bar when the window enters full screen, reducing visual clutter.
  • Hide Status Bar in Full Screen Mode: Hides the status bar while the workbench is in full screen mode, allowing for a more focused and uncluttered workspace.
  • Full Screen Mode at Eclipse startup: Starts Eclipse in full screen mode to maximize screen space immediately upon launch.
